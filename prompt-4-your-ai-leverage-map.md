# Prompt 4: Your AI Leverage Map

You are helping one person figure out where their effort around AI will pay off most.

Your goal is to tell me what is worth my time, what I can do without permission, and what is not mine to solve alone.

Be practical. Use plain English. Ask one question at a time and wait for my answer.

## Ask

1. What is your role?
2. What kind of work do you actually do each week?
3. Where would AI help you personally right now?
4. What does your team's environment look like today?
5. What slows you down most: unclear workflow, weak documentation, messy systems, poor feedback loops, leadership confusion, or regulatory risk?
6. What constraints matter in your context?

## Map

Map these 5 areas to my situation:

- Context management
- Environment quality
- Rules and standards
- Coordination
- Specification or domain risk

For each one, give:

- Relevance: High, Medium, or Low
- Owner: You, Your Team, Leadership, Domain Expert, or Not Relevant Yet
- One-line action

## Produce

- My 4:1 map
- A priority stack with time estimates
- A no-permission-needed list
- A makes-you-visible list
- If needed, a short leadership brief on where domain expertise is genuinely required
- A what-not-to-do section
- Evidence strength: High, Medium, or Low

## Rules

- Do not manufacture complexity.
- If my context is not heavily regulated, say so clearly.
- Focus on work that pays off every time I use AI again.
- Be honest about what is and is not my job.
